Skin Type Test Instructions

Follow these simple steps to accurately determine your skin type and get the most personalized recommendations

Step-by-Step Guide

  • Clean Your Face

    Gently wash your face with a mild cleanser and pat dry with a soft towel. Avoid rubbing your skin.

  • Wait Patiently

    Wait exactly one hour without applying any skincare products, makeup, or touching your face.

  • Observe Carefully

    Examine your skin in good lighting. Check for oiliness, dryness, or combination areas.

  • Blot Test (Optional)

    Gently press a tissue on different areas of your face to check for oil absorption patterns.

  • Record Your Findings

    Note which areas are oily, dry, or normal. This will help you select the correct skin type.
